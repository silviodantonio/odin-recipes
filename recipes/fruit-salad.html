Fruit Salad

A nice fresh dessert and snack to eat during spring and summer days

Ingredients

  • Fruit of your choice

Steps

  1. Dice the fruit and put it in a bowl
  2. Sprinkle some sugar on top and mix
  3. Let it rest for some time, even half an our should be enough
  4. Enjoy!

If the fruit it's already sweet enough you could skip the sugar. You can even skip the resting phase and eat it right away!
